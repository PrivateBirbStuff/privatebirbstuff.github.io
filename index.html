Harpy Jobs

You can give your harpy a job. Why? Because you said so. And because you might need more income. Your harpy may not like it much though. Each Harpy species has Synergies and Anergies, that's to say it's good at some jobs, and less good at other. Synergies provide a 25% bonus, while Anergies provide a 25% loss.

Feather Softness

Combine options for Ultimate Softness. 1-3p is normal. 4-6p is pretty soft, nice for a nap. 7-10p is godly, you'd swear she was an angel with such soft feathers. If you have above 10p, may God help your soul if you so as accidentally touch her feathers.

Bird Bath - 1p

She only bathes/showers rarely, or for very short amounts of time. 5% of income.

Rain - 2p

She takes frequent showers. 10% of income.

Soak - 3p

Every Sunday, she’ll take a nice, relaxing bath, and shower most mornings. 15% of income.

Drench - 4p

She bathes every morning, and will wash herself thoroughly. If you manage to get her to invite you, she’ll wash you too. 15% of income.

Once a month, she’ll take the day off to go to a nice, uptown spa for a full treatment. You can join her if you’re good enough friends, and it’s mixed-gender. (Can be taken along with any other option). 10% of income.

Personality and Misc.

In this section you will be provided with different options that can affect personality and other miscellaneous aspects.

Personality quirks

Personality quirks are exactly what they sound like. Choose what you like.

Hightened maternity instincts

Your harpy will want to have chicks with you quite strongly, especially during mating season. This will mostly only affect her breeding drive.

Dominant

Why'd you even get a harpy as a pet? The harpy will be dominant and always try to dominate you. Being vanilla or submissive isn't comfortable for her.

Submissive

Your harpy will be incredibly submissive and will want to be dominated by you. She will have any fetishes you have, as long as they fit the Dom and Sub dynamic. Some harpies may still prefer to get to know you first. Being vanilla or dominant isn't comfortable for her.

Polygamous

Your harpy won't at all mind you having a harem of harpies, though that says nothing about the other harpies in said harem. She also won't mind polygamous incest, if you're into that. Some harpies already don't care by default.

Total Inversion

Your harpy's starting personality will be totally inverted. An owl would become a slutty bimbo while a Robin would become your worst nightmare.

Yurification!

Your harpy will take interest in other harpies you may own, and may even be okay with a threesome. If also has the purebred quirk, she will take an interest in her own children as well.

Nest Egg

Your harpy isn't very active or aware, preferring to laze about. She might not notice what you're doing to her... +5% income.

Physical quirks

Similar to personality quirks, but affect your harpy physically.

Broodmother

Your harpy will lay eggs much more often than usual and will be more fertile. Don't use this on a chicken or you'll have more eggs than you know what to do with by the end of the day.

Purebred

Your harpy is definitely inbred, no doubt, though she shows no signs of it at all. You can inbreed as much as you like without any adverse consequences, though she may have a bit of a fetish for it...

Incest

You are your harpy's father/brother, your choice. You can also choose whether it's a blood relation or step relation. If you choose blood brother, you yourself may also need to be a harpy, but male. If you plan on romantic or sexual relations, you may need to do some convincing unless she has the purebred quirk.
